Hello there!

My name is Paul Mandia. I have been an artist almost all my life, in one form or another. I am an Illustrator, painter, and digital designer. I have an expression style of art which I use to create emotive and thoughtful compositions. The main goal with my art is to tell a story through expressive illustrations and designs. What I want to accomplish through this expressiveness is to have people feel. I am constantly building skills and using those skills to influence my artist endeavors.
